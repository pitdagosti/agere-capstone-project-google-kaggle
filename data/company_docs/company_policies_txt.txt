PROLOGWARE SOLUTIONS - COMPANY POLICIES
========================================

Company Name: PrologWare Solutions
Type: Community-Based Tech Startup
Focus Areas: Education, Technology, Entrepreneurship
Business Model: Hybrid (Community Platform + Agency Services)

========================================
REMOTE WORK POLICY
========================================

Work Arrangement: Hybrid/Flexible
Remote Work: Fully supported for all technical roles
Office Requirements: Optional coworking sessions for team collaboration
Geographic Restrictions: Open to candidates globally, preference for overlapping time zones
Equipment: Company provides necessary software licenses; hardware is employee's responsibility initially
Communication: Daily standups via virtual meetings, async updates on Slack/Discord

========================================
WORKING HOURS
========================================

Core Hours: Flexible, with 4-hour overlap window (to be determined by team)
Full-time: 40 hours/week with flexible scheduling
Part-time/Contract: Project-based or hourly arrangements available

========================================
LEAVE POLICY
========================================

Annual Leave: 15 days paid leave (after probation)
Sick Leave: 10 days paid sick leave annually
Public Holidays: As per local jurisdiction
Unpaid Leave: Available after discussion with management

========================================
PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
========================================

Learning Budget: Access to courses and certifications (budget allocated based on role)
Community Events: Free participation in all PrologWare workshops, webinars, and seminars
Skill Development: Encouraged to spend 5 hours/month on learning during work hours

========================================
COMMUNICATION & COLLABORATION
========================================

Tools: WhatsApp, Circle, Slack/Discord, Zoom/Google Meet
Response Time: Within 24 hours for non-urgent matters, 4 hours for urgent issues
Meeting Culture: Agenda-driven, recorded when possible, respect for time zones

========================================
EMPLOYEE BENEFITS
========================================

COMMUNITY BENEFITS:
- Lifetime Access: Free lifetime membership to PrologWare Solutions community
- Resources Heaven Access: Premium tier access to all courses, tools, and resources
- Networking: Priority access to exclusive events, workshops, and entrepreneur meetups
- Knowledge Sharing: Platform to share expertise through webinars and content creation

PROFESSIONAL BENEFITS:
- Portfolio Building: Credit and showcase for projects worked on
- Equity Options: Equity grants for early-stage full-time employees (terms to be discussed)
- Profit Sharing: Performance-based bonuses tied to company growth
- Recommendation Letters: Provided upon request after 6 months of employment

GROWTH OPPORTUNITIES:
- Career Path: Clear progression from junior to senior to lead roles
- Cross-functional Exposure: Opportunity to work across different service areas
- Leadership Roles: Community leadership positions for interested employees
- Startup Experience: Direct involvement in building and scaling a startup

WORK-LIFE BALANCE:
- Flexible Schedule: Results-oriented work environment
- No Micromanagement: Trust-based culture with autonomy
- Mental Health: Encouraged breaks and mental health days
- Family-Friendly: Understanding of personal commitments

========================================
COMPANY EXPECTATIONS
========================================

FROM ALL EMPLOYEES:
- Ownership: Take responsibility for your work and outcomes
- Communication: Be transparent, responsive, and proactive
- Quality: Deliver high-quality work that meets client needs
- Collaboration: Work well with team members and support each other
- Learning: Continuously improve skills and stay updated
- Community Spirit: Contribute to and engage with the PrologWare community

PERFORMANCE METRICS:
- Delivery: Meeting project deadlines and milestones
- Quality: Client satisfaction and code/work quality
- Growth: Personal skill development and contribution to team knowledge
- Engagement: Participation in community events and initiatives
- Initiative: Proactively identifying and solving problems

PROBATION PERIOD:
Duration: 3 months for full-time employees
Reviews: Monthly check-ins with feedback
Criteria: Performance, cultural fit, and mutual alignment
Outcome: Confirmation, extension, or termination based on performance

========================================
COMPENSATION STRUCTURE
========================================

SALARY BANDS (USD per month):
- Junior Developers: $500-$1,000
- Mid-Level Developers: $1,000-$2,000
- Senior Developers/Engineers: $2,000-$3,500
- Consultants/Specialists: $1,500-$3,000
- Community Managers: $600-$1,200

Note: Actual compensation based on experience, location, and project involvement

PAYMENT STRUCTURE:
- Full-time: Monthly salary
- Part-time/Contract: Hourly or project-based
- Equity: Offered to early-stage full-time employees
- Bonuses: Performance-based, tied to company revenue
