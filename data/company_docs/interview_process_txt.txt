PROLOGWARE SOLUTIONS - INTERVIEW PROCESS & SCORING GUIDE
=========================================================

=========================================================
INTERVIEW PROCESS (6 STAGES)
=========================================================

STAGE 1: APPLICATION REVIEW (Automated)
Timeline: 48-72 hours
- Resume/CV screening for required skills
- Portfolio/GitHub review
- Initial scoring based on experience and qualifications

STAGE 2: INITIAL SCREENING
Duration: 15-20 minutes
Timeline: Within 1 week of application
- Video call with HR or team lead
- Overview of candidate background
- Discussion of role expectations and company culture
- Salary expectations and availability

STAGE 3: TECHNICAL ASSESSMENT
Duration: 60-90 minutes
Timeline: 3-5 days to complete, 48 hours for review

For Developers: Coding challenge or take-home assignment
For AI Engineers: AI implementation task or system design
For Consultants: Case study or strategy presentation
For Community Managers: Content creation and scenario handling

STAGE 4: TECHNICAL INTERVIEW
Duration: 45-60 minutes
Timeline: Within 1 week of assessment completion
- Deep dive into technical skills
- Discussion of past projects and problem-solving approach
- Code review or technical architecture discussion
- Questions about the technical assignment

STAGE 5: CULTURAL FIT & FOUNDER INTERVIEW
Duration: 30-45 minutes
Timeline: Within 3-5 days of technical interview
- Meeting with founder or senior team member
- Alignment with company vision and values
- Discussion of career goals and growth expectations
- Two-way Q&A session

STAGE 6: OFFER & ONBOARDING
Timeline: Offer extended within 48 hours, start date flexible
- Offer letter with compensation details
- Contract review and signing
- Onboarding checklist and welcome package

=========================================================
SCORING GUIDE FOR INTERVIEWERS
=========================================================

RATING SCALE:

0-59: Does not meet requirements
60-69: Meets minimum requirements
70-79: Good fit for the role
80-89: Strong candidate, highly recommended
90-100: Exceptional candidate, hire immediately

=========================================================
RED FLAGS
=========================================================

- Poor communication or unprofessional behavior
- Misrepresentation of skills or experience
- Negative attitude toward learning or feedback
- Lack of interest in community or mission
- Unavailability for required collaboration times

=========================================================
GREEN FLAGS
=========================================================

- Strong portfolio demonstrating relevant skills
- Proactive communication and follow-up
- Genuine enthusiasm for the role and company
- Growth mindset and willingness to learn
- Community contributions or open-source work

=========================================================
CANDIDATE FAQ
=========================================================

Q: Is this position remote?
A: Yes, all positions are remote-friendly with flexible working hours.

Q: Do you sponsor visas?
A: Currently, we do not sponsor visas but are open to contractors globally.

Q: What is the expected start date?
A: Flexible based on candidate availability, typically 2-4 weeks after offer acceptance.

Q: Will I get equity?
A: Full-time early-stage employees are eligible for equity grants based on role and contribution.

Q: How does the community membership work?
A: All employees get lifetime premium access to Resources Heaven and community events.

Q: What growth opportunities exist?
A: We support career progression, skill development, and leadership roles as the company grows.
